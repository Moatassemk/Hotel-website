About our Hotel

El Mouradi Djerba is a well-known hotel located on the northeastern coast of the beautiful island of Djerba in Tunisia. The hotel offers a variety of accommodations, including rooms and suites equipped with modern amenities and often featuring balconies or terraces with scenic views. Guests can enjoy an array of facilities such as swimming pools, spa and wellness centers, restaurants, bars, and beach access. The hotel likely provides entertainment programs and is known for its convenient access to the beach, making it a popular choice for travelers seeking a relaxing coastal getaway.
